- **4+ years** building real products — self-taught, mostly solo and small teams
- Focused on **AI integration**, **computer vision**, and **generative bots**
- Built **real-time CV pipelines** on live camera feeds: face-recognition attendance, automated counting, presence tracking with alerts
- Shipped **AI products**: a custom-apparel marketplace with automated print fulfilment, and an AI content-generation platform
- Comfortable owning the **whole thing** — backend, integrations, generation pipelines, deploy
